Symon; (1948-2020) is one of Bali's most famous foreign artistm in the lineage of spies, Bonnet and Arie Smit. Born in Detroit, he created art in bali for 42 years. He was a master in evrey medium: painting, print-makking, sculpture, jewellery, furniturem and clothing. he gave traditional subject a pop art style- all ini his trademark flowing lines, vivid colours and wit.
